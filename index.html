OLD AGE HOME IN CAPE TOWN

Welcome to the
Ladies Christian Old Age Home

The Ladies Christian Old Age Home has been an integral part of the City of Cape Town and its environs since 1876.

It was founded out of a concern for elderly ladies who need care. We believe in respecting their rights, to accord them dignity, to attend to their welfare and to ensure their freedom from danger or fear.

Care and Comfort
at Ladies Christian Home

It remains based on Christian principles, ensuring that our elderly citizens feel valued, are well looked after, both mentally and physically and continue to enjoy their lives.

Services and Facilities

We offer the following facilities:

  • Single, Double Rooms in the Main Home
  • Hand basins and wall heaters in all rooms
  • 2/4/6 Bedded wards and 7 Private Rooms in Frail Care
  • Alzheimer’s Unit that can accommodate 14 residents (2 per room)
  • Communal bathrooms

We render the following services:

  • 24 Hour Nursing Care 7 days a week
  • Medication controlled by our senior nursing staff
  • State doctor available weekly for social pensioners
  • We reserve the right to move residents where more nursing intervention is needed
  • 3 Nutritious meals per day
  • Tea is served to frail residents at 10.30 am and 2 pm
  • Rooms cleaned 1 x week
  • Laundry
  • Hairdresser 1 x week
  • Shopping 1 x week
  • Library 1 x week
  • Tuck-shop 1 x week
  • Wellness Clinic Morning 1 x month
  • Foot-care Sister 1 x month
  • Dementia Support Group 1 x month

Rates

In all its endeavors we are motivated by our love for God and an expression of love in tender compassionate care, benevolent and charitable acts for the benefit of the physical, mental and spiritual welfare of those for whom it cares. Ladies Christian Home offers care rather than selling accommodation.

We reserve the right to move a resident when it becomes evident that they would benefit from more care. The resident and her family will be informed in advance.

  • Fees available on application
  • Application forms available at first interview (not electronically available).
  • Smoking and Drinking are strictly prohibited.
